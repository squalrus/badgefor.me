You stay home.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does BadgeFor.Me do?

We collect your physical work badge, drive it to your workplace, and scan it on your behalf. That's it. No fake activity, no digital trickery, no Slack messages typed by robots. Just a highly trained human performing a single beep.

Do I need to be home when you pick up my badge?

No. You may leave your badge in a secure, pre‑agreed location. Our drivers are trained in discreet retrieval, including mailbox extraction, porch pickup, and the classic "under the doormat" maneuver.

Who are your drivers?

Our drivers are elite professionals trained in:

  • Badge handling
  • Traffic navigation
  • Avoiding eye contact with building security
  • Maintaining plausible professionalism at all times

They are the backbone of our operation and the heroes your commute never deserved.

How do you decide when to scan my badge?

Our AI systems—PresenceGPT™ and BadgeScanAI™—analyze building access patterns, historical scan times, and traffic conditions to determine a natural, human‑like scan window.
No two scans are ever identical.
Just like real attendance.

Do you scan into multiple buildings?

Yes. Our AI may occasionally choose a different building within your campus to maintain realistic variability. This is normal. This is intentional. This is science.

Do you ever simulate work activity?

Absolutely not.

We do not:

  • Send Slack messages
  • Generate keystrokes
  • Fake mouse movement
  • Manipulate dashboards
  • Fabricate presence data

We only perform real badge scans using your real badge at your real workplace.
